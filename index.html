What I've done

I'm the cofounder of Phantom Design, a FinTech product development company that has helped to build bitcoin wallets, cryptocurrency exchanges, smart credit cards and more.

Before this, I guided product strategy at 22Seven, where I launched a ‘nudge engine’ that uses artificial intelligence to help people manage their money, worked as Strategy Director at Quirk (now Mirum), Africa’s largest digital marketing agency, and founded a startup mentorship programme for small towns called Desert Bootcamp.

I co-host a podcast called Take Back the Day, which is a productivity podcast for people who hate productivity podcasts.

I have a BA Honours Degree from the University of Cape Town, studied Strategy Design at the Gordon Institute of Business Science and was a 2014 Mandela Washington Fellow at Yale University, where I got to high-five Barack Obama (true story).

In my spare time, I make choose-your-own adventure games and write horror stories.
